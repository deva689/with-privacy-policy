Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application WITH (the “App”, “Platform”, or “Service”) operated by Zuntra Digital Private Limited (“Company”, “we”, “our”, or “us”).

WITH is intended only for users aged 18 years or older.

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your information when you use WITH.

By using the Platform, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the Platform.

2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

  • Name, age, gender, preferences, bio, interests
  • Email address or phone number used for login
  • Photos uploaded to your account
  • Messages sent through the app
  • Reports, support requests, or verification data voluntarily submitted

B. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type, OS version, app version
  • IP address and approximate region
  • App activity, crash logs, diagnostics
  • Fraud prevention and security signals

C. Optional Permissions

  • Camera / Photos access for uploads
  • Location access for nearby discovery (if enabled)
  • Push notifications for messages and matches

3. How We Use Information

  • Create and maintain accounts
  • Provide profiles, matches, chats, and recommendations
  • Detect abuse, scams, spam, fake accounts, and fraud
  • Review reports and enforce community rules
  • Provide support
  • Improve reliability, security, and app features
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Safety, Reporting, and Moderation

WITH does not tolerate harassment, threats, hate speech, scams, impersonation, or abusive behavior.

  • Users can report profiles, messages, or content inside the app.
  • Users can block other users at any time.
  • Blocked users may be removed from visibility where applicable.
  • Reported content may be reviewed manually or automatically.
  • We aim to review urgent reports promptly.
  • Accounts violating our rules may be suspended or removed.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited data with:

  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Analytics or crash reporting providers
  • Identity verification partners if used
  • Authorities when legally required
  • Corporate entities during merger or acquisition

6. Tracking and Advertising

WITH does not use third-party tracking for cross-app or cross-website targeted advertising without required user permission.

If tracking is introduced in the future on Apple platforms, we will request permission where required.

7. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary for app operation, safety, fraud prevention, dispute handling, and legal compliance.

Data Category Retention Period
Profile Data Until deletion or prolonged inactivity
Photos Removed within a reasonable time after deletion request
Messages May remain temporarily for safety or backups
Reports / Abuse Logs Up to 24 months
Crash Logs Up to 12 months

8. Your Rights and Controls

  • Access or update profile data
  • Delete your account
  • Withdraw optional permissions in device settings
  • Request support regarding your data
  • Report unsafe behavior

9. Children's Privacy

WITH is strictly for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly permit minors to use the Platform.

10. Security

  • HTTPS encrypted connections
  • Restricted administrative access
  • Monitoring for suspicious activity
  • Reasonable technical safeguards

11. International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries where our service providers operate, subject to reasonable safeguards where required by law.

12. Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page.
