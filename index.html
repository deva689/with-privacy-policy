Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect personal data when you use the WITH mobile application and related services.

By using the Platform you consent to this Privacy Policy and the processing of your personal data in accordance with applicable laws including:

If you do not agree with this policy please discontinue use of the platform.

2. Scope

  • All registered users and visitors of the WITH platform
  • All personal and sensitive personal data collected through our services
  • All services provided under the WITH brand globally

3. Data We Collect

We collect personal data and usage information to operate the platform, enable matchmaking, verify identities, ensure safety, and improve services.

A. Data You Provide

  • Account Data – Phone number used for OTP login and account verification.
  • Profile Data – Name, gender, relationship preferences, interests, and approximate location.
  • Content – Photos, messages, profile descriptions, and other content you upload.
  • Customer Support Data – Information you provide when contacting support or submitting reports.
  • Reports About Other Users – Information submitted when reporting abuse or violations.

B. Automatically Collected Data

  • Usage Data – Login activity, interactions, matches, messages, and features used.
  • Behavioural Signals – Swipe patterns, dwell time on profiles, scrolling behaviour, and interaction metrics used to improve matching algorithms.
  • Technical Data – Device type, device ID, operating system, IP address, network information, crash logs, and diagnostics.
  • Location Data – Approximate location derived from IP address.
  • Security Logs – Login attempts, suspicious behaviour, and anti-fraud signals.

C. Data Collected With Your Permission

  • Geolocation Data – If enabled, device location may be used for distance-based discovery.
  • Selfie Verification Data – Face images used to confirm identity and detect impersonation.
  • ID Verification Data – Government identification may be requested for safety verification.

D. Sensitive Personal Data

Some information may be considered sensitive under applicable laws including:

  • Gender identity
  • Sexual orientation
  • Relationship preferences
  • Biometric or selfie verification data
  • Communication content within chats

Sensitive data is processed only with explicit consent and is never used for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Provide matchmaking and recommendations
  • Enable messaging and interactions between users
  • Detect fake profiles, bots, and impersonation
  • Prevent fraud, harassment, and abuse
  • Respond to customer support requests
  • Improve platform features and algorithms
  • Conduct internal research using anonymised data
  • Comply with legal obligations

5. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data based on:

  • Performance of a contract when providing services
  • Your consent when processing sensitive personal data
  • Legitimate interests including improving services and ensuring platform safety
  • Compliance with legal obligations

6. Consent

By registering for WITH you provide consent for the collection and processing of your personal data in accordance with this policy and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

You may withdraw consent at any time through account settings or by contacting us. Withdrawal may limit certain services.

Data is stored on secure cloud servers located in India and other jurisdictions with adequate data protection safeguards.

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to provide services, ensure safety, comply with legal obligations, and prevent abuse.

Inactive accounts may be deleted after two years of inactivity.
