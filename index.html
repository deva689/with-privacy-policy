Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application WITH (the “App”, “Platform”, or “Service”) operated by Zuntra Digital Private Limited (“Company”, “we”, “our”, or “us”).

WITH is an adults-only social discovery platform intended only for users aged 18 years or older.

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect personal data when you use the WITH mobile application and related services.

By using the Platform you consent to this Privacy Policy and applicable laws. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the Platform.

2. Data We Collect

A. Information You Provide

  • Name, age, gender, preferences, bio, interests
  • Phone number or email used for login
  • Photos uploaded to your profile
  • Messages sent through the app
  • Reports or support requests
  • Verification content if submitted voluntarily

B. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type, operating system, app version
  • IP address and approximate location
  • App activity, crashes, diagnostics
  • Safety signals used to detect fraud or abuse

C. Optional Permissions

  • Camera / Photos access for profile uploads
  • Location access for nearby discovery features
  • Notifications for matches and messages

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Show compatible profiles and recommendations
  • Enable chats, likes, and matches
  • Detect fake profiles, spam, bots, scams, abuse
  • Review reports and enforce community rules
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve features, performance, and security
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Safety, Reporting, and Moderation

WITH does not tolerate harassment, hate speech, nudity involving minors, threats, scams, impersonation, or abusive behavior.

  • Users can Report profiles, messages, or content inside the app.
  • Users can Block other users at any time.
  • Blocked users are removed from visibility where technically applicable.
  • Reported content may be reviewed manually or automatically.
  • We aim to review urgent reports quickly and generally within 24 hours.
  • Accounts violating rules may be restricted, suspended, or permanently removed.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share limited data only with:

  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Analytics or crash reporting vendors
  • Identity verification vendors if used
  • Law enforcement when legally required
  • Corporate entities during merger or acquisition

6. Tracking and Advertising

WITH does not use third-party tracking for cross-app or cross-website advertising without required user permission.

If tracking is introduced in the future on Apple platforms, we will request consent through Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework where required.

7. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary for service operation, safety, fraud prevention, dispute handling, and legal compliance.

Data Category Retention Period
Profile Data Until account deletion or prolonged inactivity
Photos Removed within a reasonable period after deletion request
Messages May remain temporarily for safety, backup, or dispute review
Reports / Abuse Logs Up to 24 months
Crash / Diagnostics Logs Up to 12 months

8. Your Rights and Controls

  • Access or update profile information
  • Delete your account
  • Withdraw optional permissions in device settings
  • Request support regarding your data
  • Report abuse or unsafe behavior

9. Children's Privacy

WITH is strictly for users aged 18+. We do not knowingly allow minors to use the platform. Accounts suspected to belong to minors may be removed.

10. Security

  • Encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Restricted administrative access
  • Monitoring for suspicious activity
  • Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards

11. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries where our providers operate, subject to reasonable safeguards where required by law.

12. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised date.
