Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how WIVIY collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects your personal data when you use our website, mobile application, or related services (collectively, the "Platform").

By using the Platform, you consent to this Policy and our compliance with:

  • California Consumer Privacy Act (US)

If you do not agree with this Policy, please discontinue use of the Platform.

2. Scope

This Policy applies to:

  • All registered users, visitors, and subscribers ("Users").
  • All personal data and sensitive personal data that we collect, whether online or offline.
  • Websites, apps, events and other services we operate under the WIVIY brand globally ("Service" and/or "Services").

This Policy applies globally. Indian users are protected under the DPDP Act, 2023. Users in the EU, UK, or California are additionally protected under GDPR, UK-GDPR, and CCPA respectively.

3. Data We Collect

We collect personal data and usage information to operate the Platform, enable matchmaking, ensure user safety, verify identities, personalise your experience, and provide core communication features. We may also receive information about you from third parties such as Google, Facebook, or Apple sign-in, verification partners, or analytics providers. This data is collected and processed strictly for the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy and in accordance with the DPDP Act 2023, GDPR, CCPA, and applicable international laws.

A. Data You Give Us

  • Account Data: Phone number, email address, and date of birth when you create an account.
  • Profile Data: Gender, interests, preferences, approximate location, and other details. Some data may be considered sensitive (e.g. sexual orientation, health, racial or ethnic origins, religious or political beliefs). Providing such data constitutes your consent to its use as described here.
  • Content: Photos, videos, audio, text, and chats shared while using the Service.
  • Purchase Data: Transaction details including what was purchased, date, and price. Payment card or financial data if paying directly with us.
  • Marketing, Survey and Research Data: Information provided when participating in surveys, focus groups, promotions, or contests.
  • Third-Party Data: Information about others you share with us (e.g. contact details used for a feature, or reports involving another member).
  • Customer Support Data: Information provided when contacting us, including reports filed by or about you, and data collected during moderation investigations. If you were banned from another Zuntra Digital service, your data may be shared with us to take necessary actions. Where legally allowed, we may also receive information about suspected or convicted bad actors from third parties.

B. Data Generated or Automatically Collected

  • Usage Data: Activity logs, features used, interactions with other users, ads interacted with, swipe patterns, dwell time on profiles, match preferences, and behavioural signals used for matchmaking algorithms. Also metadata for spam detection, fraud prevention, and bot identification such as abnormal usage patterns, repeated failed login attempts, or unusual interaction frequency.
  • Technical Data: IP address, device ID and type, app settings, crash data, advertising identifiers (IDFA/AAID), and diagnostic/performance data (battery usage, network quality, crash logs). Approximate location may be derived from your IP address even without GPS permission. You may reset advertising identifiers from your device settings at any time.

C. Data Collected with Your Permission

  • Geolocation Data: Precise latitude/longitude if you grant permission. Declining may limit discovery and nearby-user features. Approximate location may still be derived from your IP address for safety and fraud-prevention purposes. You may withdraw geolocation permission at any time through your device settings.
  • Face Geometry Data: Collected for Selfie Verification to detect impersonation or fake profiles. Stored only for the verification period and deleted within 30 days unless required for safety investigations. Declining may limit access to trust badges or advanced safety features. Trusted third-party verification partners may process this data under strict contractual obligations.
  • ID Data: Government-issued ID for identity verification, collected via upload, camera capture, or third-party KYC providers. Retained only for the period necessary for verification or compliance obligations.

D. Sensitive Personal Data (DPDP Act & GDPR)

We collect and process sensitive information only with your explicit consent, including:

  • Gender, sexual orientation, and relationship preferences
  • Biometric or facial data (for identity or photo verification)
  • Communication content within chats (end-to-end encrypted where possible)
  • Optional health or lifestyle information (if voluntarily shared)

We do not use sensitive data for advertising, cross-platform marketing, or any purpose not expressly stated in this Policy. You may refuse to provide sensitive personal data; however, certain personalised features may not function fully. You may withdraw consent at any time via account settings. Withdrawal does not affect processing already lawfully carried out.

We do not collect financial or criminal record data unless necessary for verified identity programs or required by law.

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Service, including matchmaking and personalisation.
  • Keep all Users safe and secure while using our Services.
  • Show relevant ads without using sensitive personal data for advertising or marketing purposes.
  • Create and maintain your account and platform features.
  • Organise sweepstakes and contests.
  • Respond to your requests and questions.
  • Monitor service functioning, troubleshoot, and fix issues.
  • Enable purchases, process payments, and offer discounts or promotions.
  • Facilitate matchmaking and recommendation algorithms.
  • Run, measure, and improve advertising and marketing campaigns.
  • Conduct research, including developing new features using machine learning (using anonymised or pseudonymised data only).
  • Prevent, detect, and fight fraud, abuse, and violations of our Terms — including preventing re-registration by banned users.
  • Verify identity and secure payments against financial fraud.
  • Comply with legal obligations, respond to lawful law enforcement requests, and defend legal claims.
  • Enable messaging, in-app interactions, and personalised content suggestions.

We do not sell your personal data to advertisers or third parties. Sensitive personal data is never used for targeted advertising.

Legal grounds for processing include: performance of contract, your consent, our legitimate interests (service improvement, safety, and promoting the platform), protection of vital interests, and compliance with legal obligations.

If we transfer your personal data outside of India, we obtain your consent where required and ensure compliance with applicable transfer safeguards. We maintain a record of your consents, withdrawals, and preferences for compliance and audit purposes.

11. Security Practices

We employ industry-standard technical and organisational security measures including:

  • End-to-end encryption for chats and encryption at rest for stored data where applicable.
  • HTTPS and TLS protection for all data transmission.
  • Access controls, firewalls, and intrusion monitoring.
  • Regular audits and vulnerability assessments.
  • Zero-trust security architecture with strict role-based access controls (RBAC).
  • Multi-factor authentication and credential-security standards for internal systems.
  • Continuous monitoring for bot attacks, suspicious behaviour, deepfake attempts, and fraudulent activity.
  • Vendor and third-party security reviews to ensure equivalent protections across processors.
  • Employee confidentiality agreements and mandatory security training.

No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We encourage Users to exercise caution when sharing personal or sensitive information on the Platform. In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected Users and relevant authorities including the Data Protection Board of India, as required by applicable law.

12. Children's Data

If we become aware that a minor has registered, we will immediately delete their data and disable the account in compliance with the DPDP Act 2023 and COPPA (US). Where required by law, we report child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or other child-safety concerns to law-enforcement authorities and relevant agencies. Underage accounts detected are permanently blocked to prevent re-registration.
